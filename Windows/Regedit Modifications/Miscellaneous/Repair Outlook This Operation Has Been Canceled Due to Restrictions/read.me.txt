Repair Outlook This Operation Has Been Canceled Due to Restrictions 1.0

This batch file is designed to resolve the "This Operation Has Been Canceled Due to Restrictions" error in Microsoft Outlook.

It will make entries in your registry. You are strongly advised to back up your registry before running this file. MajorGeeks accepts no responsibility for any consequences that may arise from running this file.

To use, simply click the batch file. The registry entries will be made automatically. Afterwards, restart Outlook to check if the repair was successful.
